Easy to cook, easy to serve!

For every cup of Saffola Oats, add 1 ½ cups of water in a pan

Cook on medium flame for 3 minutes or microwave for 3 minutes on full power. Stir occasionally

Serve hot to enjoy the distinct flavours. Garnish with veggies, herbs and choice of toppings.

Ingredients:

Saffola Peppy Tomato oats - 40 g

Saffola Active oil - 2 tbsp 5ml

Cumin seeds - 1 tsp 2g

Carrot - 1/2 cub 100 g

Capsicum & Onion - 1/2 cup 50g each

Spinach Tortilla - 2 x 100 g

Method:

1. Follow the directions to cook Saffola Peppy Tomato oats and set them aside to cool

2. Heat Saffola active oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, capsicum, onion, salt to taste, and black pepper. Cook for 3-4 minutes on low flame.

3. Keep a spinach tortilla on a plate and spread some oats on top.
